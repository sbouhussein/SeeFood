Description

This app allows users to take/upload pictures of food labels, from which it will then extract their nutritional information. It is geared towards individuals who want to improve/track their nutrition information and health habits. Instead of the user having to track every single item they eat, the app would provide them with a visual display to see how the serving size of the food plays into a healthy, daily diet, customized to the users' age, weight, and height. The apps key function will be processing images of the food labels and calculating useful statistics for the user. SeeFood was developed by Arina Golikova, Sami Bouhussein, and Chinhnam Nguyen.
